Our Story

In the bustling heart of Indore, nestled amidst the fragrant spice markets and clanging streetcars, resided Dwarika Restaurant. Established decades ago by a young couple, Maya and Dhruv, Dwarika wasn't your typical fine-dining establishment. It began as a humble dhaba, a roadside eatery, where the aroma of freshly ground spices and sizzling tandoori grills enticed passersby.
